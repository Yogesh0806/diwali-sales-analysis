# 📊 Diwali Sales Analysis

An Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project on the **Diwali Sales Dataset** using Python. This project analyzes customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and sales trends to uncover business insights that can help improve marketing strategies and decision-making.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to analyze Diwali sales data and identify:

- Customer purchasing patterns
- Top-performing states and product categories
- Customer demographics
- High-value customer segments
- Business insights for targeted marketing

---

## 📂 Dataset

- **Dataset Name:** Diwali Sales Data.csv
- **Rows:** 11,251
- **Columns:** 15
- **Format:** CSV

The dataset contains information about customers, including:

- User ID
- Gender
- Age Group
- Marital Status
- State
- Occupation
- Product Category
- Orders
- Amount
- Product ID
- City
- Zone

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### 1. Data Cleaning
- Imported the dataset
- Checked dataset shape
- Inspected data types
- Removed unnecessary columns
- Handled missing values
- Converted columns into appropriate data types

### 2. Gender Analysis
- Number of male vs female customers
- Total sales by gender

**Insight:**
> Female customers contribute the highest sales and purchasing power.

---

### 3. Age Group Analysis
- Distribution of customers across age groups
- Sales by age group and gender

**Insight:**
> Customers aged **26–35 years**, especially females, are the most active buyers.

---

### 4. State-wise Analysis
- Orders by state
- Revenue by state

**Top States**
- Uttar Pradesh
- Maharashtra
- Karnataka

---

### 5. Marital Status Analysis

**Insight:**
> Married women have the highest purchasing power.

---

### 6. Occupation Analysis

Top occupations include:

- IT
- Healthcare
- Aviation

These professionals contribute the largest share of purchases.

---

### 7. Product Category Analysis

Most purchased categories:

- Food
- Clothing
- Electronics

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

- Female customers spend more than male customers.
- Customers aged **26–35 years** are the primary buyers.
- Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka generate the highest sales.
- Married women are the most valuable customer segment.
- IT, Healthcare, and Aviation professionals purchase the most products.
- Food, Clothing, and Electronics are the best-selling product categories.

---

## 🎯 Conclusion

The analysis reveals that the ideal target customer is:

> **Married women aged 26–35 years from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation, who are more likely to purchase products from Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.**

These insights can help businesses create targeted marketing campaigns, improve customer engagement, and maximize sales during festive seasons.
